# Threats to Validity
- **Construct Validity:** Are we truly measuring "entity awareness" via the multitask objective?
- **Internal Validity:** The Translation Pipeline might be using an inferior translation API (Google Translate) compared to SOTA NMT models (e.g., NLLB-200), slightly inflating our comparative advantage.
- **External Validity:** Agglutinative morphology findings (Swahili) may not perfectly map to tonal isolating languages.\n
